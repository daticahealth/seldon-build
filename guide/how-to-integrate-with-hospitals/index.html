How to Integrate with Hospitals and Health Systems

The definitive integration guide written by industry experts

Integration is a journey, not an API or easily solved by consultants. It is a multi-step process laden with missteps and misinformation. It’s also confusing, so you might be asking yourself: How do I integrate with a hospital or health system?

This guide is designed to be an educational piece for those just getting started with healthcare integration. It outlines the typical path, the technology required, and the players involved. It also includes a free project plan in the form of a downloadable spreadsheet.

To learn more about how to integrate with hospitals and health systems, be sure to check out our Academy.
