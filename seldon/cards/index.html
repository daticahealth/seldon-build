Cards

Card patterns with lots of variations.

Card variations

These cards demonstrate a variety of configurations. They leverage and extend the Foundation 6.3 Card pattern. They have sections, and are set to distribute the sections evenly, allowing equal-height cards to have "sticky" footers.

To ensure the cards stretch to fit a block-grid, be sure to add .row--card-grid in the containing .row.
