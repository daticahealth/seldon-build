Careers

Help us help healthcare adopt the cloud

See open positions

Datica has offices in Madison and Seattle, but we are also a distributed team, with members working across 10+ states. We value team members who contribute and thrive, regardless of geography and timezone.

Benefits

  • Medical and Dental Plan, Datica covers 100% of single premiums, and the majority of premiums for family coverage.
  • Flex Spending Accounts offered (both medical and dependent care).
  • A 401k plan, as well as stock options.
  • We have an open vacation policy that allows for unlimited planned time off, as well as personal days as needed.
  • Open Vacation Policy
  • Awesome swag at different intervals as well.

Work style

  • Super flexible working hours.
  • Agile development with two week sprints and daily standups.
  • Central communication is done via Slack.
  • Full internal transparency, monthly company virtual meetings.
  • Time periods for personal learning and growth.
  • Autonomous work with regular check-ins.
  • We celebrate achievements together, both large and small.

Datica is always looking for talented new team members. If you are curious about career opportunities with Datica, please email Maggie Ostrowski at maggie@datica.com.
